Q-19: what is the diffrent betwwen HTML & XHTML?

Ans:-


(1)XHTML:-
-Stands for Xtensible Hyper Text Markup Lenguage.
-Stricter then Html.
-Support to all browser.
-Beginners find it difficult to understand.
-XHTML provides more security.

(2)HTML:-
-Stand for Hyper Text Markup Lenguage.
-It has simple syntax and can be understood easily.
-HTML is less secure.
